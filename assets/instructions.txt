How to customize your webpage:

1. **Add your picture:**
   Rename your favorite picture of her (or both of you) to `our_picture.jpg` and place it in this `assets` folder.

2. **Add your song (optional):**
   Rename your favorite song file to `our_song.mp3` and place it in this `assets` folder. The webpage will automatically play it! (If you don't add it, it will just play the default soft romantic song).

Enjoy! ❤️
